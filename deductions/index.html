Self-employed deductions

Understand expense categories before estimating

Advertisement

A potentially deductible business expense generally needs a valid business purpose and adequate records. Personal expenses do not become business expenses merely because you are self-employed.

Common categories to review

  • Supplies, software, and professional services
  • Qualified travel and business mileage
  • Potential home-office expenses
  • Insurance, retirement, and education items subject to specific rules
