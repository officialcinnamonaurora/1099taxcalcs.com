    Clarity, sourcing, and corrections

    Review standards

    • No invented rates, limits, deadlines, eligibility conclusions, credentials, reviews, or outcome claims.

    • Profession and expense pages must contain facts specific to their subject rather than keyword substitutions.

    Corrections

    Corrections should be reflected in source data, generated pages, related calculators or guides, tests, and the visible review date. The contact page explains the current owner-configuration status.
