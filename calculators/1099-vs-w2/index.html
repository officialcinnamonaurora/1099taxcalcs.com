    How this estimate works

    The 1099 side subtracts business expenses, health-insurance difference, and a payroll-tax difference. The W-2 side adds employer benefits, paid time off, and retirement match to salary. Income taxes are excluded because they require shared household assumptions.

    Assumptions

    • If no payroll difference is entered, the tool estimates 1099 SE tax minus employee FICA on W-2 salary with annually reviewed constants.
    • All benefit values are user estimates.

    Limitations

    Classification is governed by facts and law, not preference or this comparison. This model excludes unemployment protection, workers’ compensation, risk, equipment, tax deductions, vesting, and many noncash differences.
