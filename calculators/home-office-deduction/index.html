How this estimate works

The simplified estimate multiplies the smaller of office area or your entered limit by the entered rate. The actual estimate adds eligible direct expenses to indirect expenses multiplied by the office-to-home area percentage.

Assumptions

  • All rate and limit values are user-entered and require annual verification.
  • Direct expenses are treated as fully allocable; indirect expenses are allocated by square footage.

Limitations

Regular and exclusive use, principal-place-of-business rules, income limitations, depreciation, carryovers, daycare rules, and expense classification are not evaluated. A larger estimate is not automatically the permitted choice.
