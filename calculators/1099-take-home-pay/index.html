How this estimate works

Take-home pay equals gross income minus business expenses, estimated self-employment tax, user-rate federal and state reserves, retirement contribution, and health-insurance expense. Federal and state estimates are kept distinct.

Assumptions

  • User-entered rates are simple effective-rate planning assumptions.
  • Retirement and health-insurance entries are treated as cash outflows, not fully modeled tax adjustments.

Limitations

This is a cash-planning estimate. It does not calculate tax brackets, local taxes, credits, deduction eligibility, contribution limits, or the interaction among deductions.
