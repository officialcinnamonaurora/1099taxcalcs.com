    How this estimate works

    The mileage estimate multiplies qualified business miles by your entered rate and adds eligible parking and tolls. The optional actual method adds the entered deductible actual expenses to parking and tolls for a simple comparison.

    Assumptions

    • No default mileage rate is supplied; it must be verified for the applicable tax year.
    • Parking and tolls are assumed eligible and not already included elsewhere.

    Limitations

    Commuting generally differs from qualified business mileage. Eligibility, method switching, depreciation, vehicle ownership, substantiation, and mixed use can change the deduction. Keep contemporaneous records and consult current IRS guidance.
