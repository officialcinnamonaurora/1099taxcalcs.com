    How this estimate works

    The model estimates self-employment tax, applies your chosen federal rate to a simplified income base, subtracts expected withholding and credits, and divides any positive remainder equally by four.

    Assumptions

    • Payments are displayed as four equal planning amounts.
    • No dates or safe-harbor thresholds are supplied because they require current authoritative review.
    • Federal rate is entirely user-entered.

    Limitations

    Actual estimated payments depend on current deadlines, prior-year liability, annualization, deductions, credits, other household income, withholding, and special rules. Confirm amounts and dates with current IRS Form 1040-ES instructions.
